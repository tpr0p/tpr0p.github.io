Projects
  • QOC
    • Unlike classical computers that are controlled digitally, quantum computers are controlled using analog control pulses (typically electromagnetic waves). Quantum Optimal Control finds control pulses that achieve specific transformations on a quantum system while satisfying experimental constraints. The main contribution of this tool is that it can model the decoherence of the quantum system, enabling experimentalists to find control pulses that work despite the system's decay.
