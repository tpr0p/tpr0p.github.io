Thomas Propson
I am a first-year PhD student at MIT. My current research focus is using integrated photonics to control quantum computers based on atoms. I am advised by Dirk Englund and Misha Lukin. I completed my undergrad at UChicago where I was advised by David Schuster and Fred Chong.
