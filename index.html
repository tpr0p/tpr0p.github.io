Thomas Propson
I'm a fourth-year PhD student at MIT. My research focuses on developing scalable optical technologies for quantum computing on atomic qubits. I'm advised by Dirk Englund. I completed my undergrad at UChicago where I was advised by David Schuster and Fred Chong.
